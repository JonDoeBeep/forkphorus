Credits

forkphorus is a fork of phosphorus by Nathan. Its CPS-style compilation and overall design was inspired by Rhys's sb2.js. It would have more bugs if not for Truman and Tim. The JSZip library is used to read .sb2 and .sb3 files, and the canvg library to render SVGs in <canvas> elements.
